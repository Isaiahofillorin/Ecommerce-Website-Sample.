BiZT Return Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2024

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns for most items within 30 days of receipt. To be eligible for a return, your item must:

  • Be in its original condition and packaging.
  • Not be a final sale or excluded item (e.g., perishable goods, custom orders).
  • Include the original receipt or proof of purchase.

2. How to Return an Item

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at biztsupport@gmail.com to initiate the return process.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Include the original receipt and a note with your order number and reason for return.
  4. Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service team.

3. Refunds and Exchanges

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund or exchange as follows:

  • Refunds: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 3-5 working days. Shipping fees are non-refundable.
  • Exchanges: If you wish to exchange an item, please indicate this when you contact customer service. We will process the exchange and send you a replacement item as soon as possible.

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at biztsupport@gmail.com. We will arrange for a replacement or refund and cover any return shipping costs for damaged or defective items.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable and cannot be returned for a refund or exchange. These items include:

  • Gift cards
  • Utensils Orders
  • Items marked as clerance sales
